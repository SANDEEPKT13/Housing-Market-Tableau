# 🏠 Housing Market Analysis using Tableau

## 📊 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Housing Market Analysis Dashboard** built using **Tableau**. The goal of the project is to explore housing market trends by analyzing house prices, renovation status, house age, and structural features such as bedrooms, bathrooms, and floors.

The dashboard helps users understand how different factors influence housing prices and provides insights through visual analytics.

---

## 📂 Dataset Information

The dataset used for this project is:

**File:** `Transformed_Housing_Data2.csv`

**Total Records:** 21,609 houses

### Key Attributes in Dataset

* Sale Price
* Age of House (in Years)
* Years Since Renovation
* Number of Bedrooms
* Number of Bathrooms
* Number of Floors
* Basement Area (Sqft)
* Living Area
* Lot Area
* Renovation Status

---

## 📈 Visualizations Created

### 1️⃣ Count of Transformed Housing Data

Displays the **total number of houses in the dataset**.
This KPI card provides a quick overview of the data volume used for analysis.

---

### 2️⃣ Average Sale Prices

Shows the **average housing sale price** across the dataset.
This metric helps understand the general pricing trend of the housing market.

---

### 3️⃣ Area of House from Basement (in Sqft)

Represents the **total basement area across all houses** in square feet.
This visualization helps analyze the contribution of basement space in housing properties.

---

### 4️⃣ Total Sales by Years Since Renovation

This visualization shows **housing sales distribution based on years since renovation**.
It helps understand how renovation timing affects house sale prices.

---

### 5️⃣ Distribution of House Age by Renovation Status

A **pie chart visualization** showing how house ages are distributed depending on whether the house has been renovated or not.

This provides insights into:

* Which house ages are more frequently renovated
* Renovation patterns across different house ages

---

### 6️⃣ House Age Distribution by Number of Bathrooms, Bedrooms, and Floors

This bar chart compares **house age with structural features**, including:

* Number of bathrooms
* Number of bedrooms
* Number of floors

This helps analyze how house features relate to property age.

---

## 🔎 Utilization of Filters

Filters were applied to highlight important data points and simplify analysis.

### Top N Sale Price Filter

A **Top 25 filter** was applied to the *Sale Price* field using:

**By Field → Age of House (Sum)**

This helps highlight the most significant price values in the dataset.

---

### Top N Age of House Filter

A **Top 10 filter** was applied to *Age of House (in Years)* using the field:

**Ever Renovated (Yes)**

This highlights the most common house ages where renovations occurred.

---

### House Age Distribution Top N Filter

A **Top 5 filter** was applied to house ages based on **record counts**, focusing the visualization on the most frequent house age groups.

---

## 📊 Dashboard

A **comprehensive dashboard** was created to bring together all visualizations into a single view.

### Dashboard Components

* Count of Houses
* Average Sale Price
* Total Basement Area
* Total Sales by Years Since Renovation
* House Age Distribution
* Structural Feature Analysis

The dashboard allows users to explore housing trends and relationships between different variables.

---

## 📖 Storyboard

A Tableau **Story** was created to present the analysis step-by-step.

### Story Flow

1. Introduction to Housing Market Trends
2. Impact of Renovation on Sales
3. Age Distribution of Houses
4. Relationship between House Age and Structural Features

The storyboard helps guide users through the insights in a clear narrative format.
